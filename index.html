HEAD OF FOOD

Head of Food will have the chance to coordinate all the food for the evening. Responsibilities include: Working with College catering staff to plan a menu for the dining part of the evening • Liaising with the Treasurer as to costs of dining • Organise the food for the ents tickets later on in the evening • Liaising with the Ball Supervisor on the night to make sure that all food for dining and ents follows the schedule

HEAD OF PERSONNEL

Head of Personnel will have the chance to manage the recruiting of personnel to help out with the ball on the evening. Responsibilities include: Opening and running applications for jobs on the evening of the night itself • Making sure that all individuals that have signed up to work on the night of the ball are fully briefed and arrive on the night of the ball • Liaising with the Treasurer as to the costs of personnel on the night and making sure that all helpers are paid after the ball

HEAD OF DECORATIONS

The Head of Decorations will have the chance to transform the College for the evening! Responsibilities include: Providing the ideas and creativity to decorate the ball around the theme • Working with the Music and Entertainment teams to build a cohesive theme • Planning all decorations and purchasing materials • Decorating the tables for the marquee • Organising a schedule for creating decorations and decorating College • Lead a team to ensure the Ball is decorated effectively • Liaising with the Treasurers to manage the Decorations budget

HEAD OF ENTERTAINMENT

The Head of Entertainment will have the chance to co-ordinate all of the entertainment available throughout the night. Responsibilities include: Booking and confirming all Entertainment, making sure that we have detailed contacts with them all • Liaising with the Treasurer to make sure all Entertainment providers are paid on time • Ensuring the Ball Supervisor (who runs everything on the night) is fully briefed on the Entertainment schedule to ensure everything runs smoothly on the night • Composing a full programme for the night’s entertainment

HEAD OF MARKETING AND SPONSORSHIP

The Head of Marketing is responsible for ensuring the event is well advertised and attended! Responsibilities include: Designing and ordering stash (Exec hoodies and t-shirts) • Working with the Graphic Designer to create all posters, tickets, wristbands and programmes • Contact possible businesses to explore sponsorship opportunities (personal connections to businesses would be beneficial to an application to this role) • Create a sponsorship package for the Ball • Advertising the ball via social media and across college • Distributing tickets • Designing table plans

BALL SUPERVISOR

The person responsible for making sure everything goes smoothly on the night. Responsible for getting the best deals by establishing relationships with local businesses: Ensuring the event runs swiftly on the night • Investigating business deals with local companies for the ball • Attend Exec meetings and offer a creative input. • Reviewing and checking Entertainment and Decoration hire contracts • Recruiting and appointing a Assistant Events co-ordinator to work with you on the night • Attending college officer meetings when required

HEAD OF MUSIC

The Head of Music will have the chance to choose and co-ordinate the Music for the evening. Responsibilities include: Booking and confirming all Music, making sure that we have detailed contacts with them all • Liaising with the Treasurer to make sure all Music acts are paid on time • Ensuring the Ball Supervisor (who runs everything on the night) is fully briefed on the Music schedule to ensure everything runs smoothly on the night • Composing a full programme for the night’s entertainment • Working with the Decorations and Entertainment teams to build a cohesive theme • Liaising with the Treasurers to manage the Music budget

SECRETARY

The Secretary will have a significant influence on how the Ball will be run making it a very important and exciting position! Responsibilities include: Attending meetings, taking and distributing minutes to attendees • Organising the seating plan and ticket sign up • Co-ordinating ticket and invitation production and distribution with the Publicity Co-ordinator • Organising accommodation and food for performers if necessary
